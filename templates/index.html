Tileset Assembler

Your PT levels go slow due to complex tilesets?
Worry no more! Because with this tool you can make a full image of the room!
Submit the JSON files of a room and the PNG files of the respective tilesets and get an image of the room.
*it ignores the tiles on the secret layer.





Collision Hider

Got lots of visible collision in your level?
Worry no more! Because with this tool you can make them invisible!
Submit the JSON files of a room and get the new JSON of the room.
